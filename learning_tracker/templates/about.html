StudyTracker

A modern web application designed to help learners stay organized and motivated while mastering new skills.

What is StudyTracker?

StudyTracker helps you track your daily progress, set goals, celebrate milestones, and keep all your learning resources in one central location. Whether you're learning programming, languages, or any other skill, StudyTracker gives you the tools to stay organized and motivated.

Built for learners who want to:

  • Track progress across multiple subjects
  • Visualize improvement with charts and analytics
  • Stay motivated with achievement tracking
  • Organize learning resources efficiently
